Near by locations

Lucknow

The capital of Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow is scenic, lively, green, and clean, with a rich cultural life. Known for its historical monuments, the city is a major center of attraction for tourists from far and wide. It is probably the only city that enables an authentic overview of the stages of development of Indo-Islamic and Awadhi architecture.

Kanpur

Kanpur has a growing healthcare sector with key hospitals like GSVM Medical College and Lala Lajpat Rai Hospital. The city offers advanced treatments in cardiology, orthopedics, and neurology. Focused on medical education and research, Kanpur provides quality healthcare services to residents and patients from surrounding areas, playing a vital role in meeting regional healthcare needs.

New Delhi

Delhi is a major medical hub, home to top hospitals like AIIMS and Safdarjung Hospital. The city offers a wide range of specialties, including advanced treatments, surgeries, and diagnostics. Known for medical research and education, Delhi attracts patients from across India and abroad, providing quality healthcare services and cutting-edge technology in a variety of medical fields.

Mumbai

Famous for its vibrant fashion industry, Mumbai is the western coastal metropolis of Maharashtra. The city is bordered by Thane District in the North, Raigad District in the South, the Arabian Sea to the West, and Navi Mumbai to the East. Known for its film industry, Bollywood, and financial significance, Mumbai is a bustling urban hub. The city attracts people from across the globe, offering a unique blend of modernity and cultural diversity.
